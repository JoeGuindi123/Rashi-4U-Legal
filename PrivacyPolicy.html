Privacy Policy

Program: Rashi-4U

Last Updated: 4/20/2026

Information We Collect

When you use Rashi-4U, we collect:

  • Your phone number, provided automatically by your carrier when you text our service
  • The content of SMS messages you send to our service
  • Timestamps of your messages and responses

How We Use Your Information

We use this information solely to:

  • Respond to your Rashi lookup requests
  • Maintain short-term session context (for example, to disambiguate between multiple matching segments when you reply with a selection number)
  • Diagnose errors and improve the accuracy of our lookup and matching logic

Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your phone number or message content with any third parties for marketing purposes. We do not use your data for advertising.

We use Twilio, Inc. as our SMS service provider. Messages are transmitted through Twilio's infrastructure subject to their privacy policy, available at https://www.twilio.com/legal/privacy.

Data Retention

We retain message logs for up to 60 days for debugging, service improvement, and abuse prevention, after which they are deleted or anonymized. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us at the address below.

Your Rights

  • You may opt out at any time by replying STOP to any message from our service.
  • You may request deletion of data we hold about you by emailing us.
  • You may request a copy of the data we hold about you.

Children's Privacy

Rashi-4U is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last Updated" date at the top reflects the most recent version.
