Program Description

Rashi-4U is an SMS-based educational service that returns segments of Rashi's commentary on the Chumash (Pentateuch) in response to user queries containing a verse reference and a Hebrew or transliterated word. The service is intended for study and educational purposes.

Message Frequency

Message frequency varies based on your requests. You will generally receive one reply per message you send.

Message and Data Rates

Message and data rates may apply. Check with your mobile carrier for details about your messaging plan.

How to Get Help

For help, reply HELP to any message from our service, or email josephaguindi@gmail.com.

How to Opt Out

To unsubscribe at any time, reply STOP to any message. You will receive one confirmation message and will not receive any further messages from our service unless you opt back in.

Supported Carriers

Rashi-4U works with all major US carriers, including AT&T, T-Mobile, Verizon, and most MVNOs. Carriers are not liable for delayed or undelivered messages.
